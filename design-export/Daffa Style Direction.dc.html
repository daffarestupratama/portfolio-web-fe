Liquid-glass on a calm azure field

Pronounced frosted surfaces float over a soft, light, blue-tinted backdrop. Glass is used as accent — nav, hero panel, cards — never the whole page. Formal yet warm; modern, not flashy.

Motion principles
Ambient, not busy
Backdrop blobs drift over 26–32s. Hero graph repels the cursor gently.
Tactile feedback
Every interactive surface answers a pointer within 250ms — lift, tilt, glow, press.
Depth through light
Inner highlight + outer shadow shift together so glass feels lit, not flat.
Graceful degradation
